About Me

My data journey in a nutshell ;)

Hey there, I'm Darshil, a 20-year-old data enthusiast from India. I'm currently diving deep into the world of Data Science while pursuing my B.Tech in Information Technology. My journey began with simple data projects and slowly evolved into full-fledged machine learning applications—now, I’m working on everything from regression models to AI-powered web apps.

My main goal in Data Science is simple: to turn raw data into real-world impact. Whether it's predicting stock trends, analyzing user behavior, or visualizing insights through dashboards, I love finding stories hidden in the numbers. I also enjoy sharing what I learn—sometimes through code, sometimes through projects that solve actual problems.

When I'm not experimenting with models or tuning hyperparameters, you'll probably find me exploring AI research, building cool side projects, or gaming to refresh my mind.
